At Harvik Design & Engineering, we are always on the lookout for talented individuals who share our passion for mechanical technologies and innovation. We believe that our people are our greatest asset, and we are committed to fostering a collaborative and dynamic work environment.

Current Openings:

Mechanical Design Engineer

Number of Openings: 2

Designation: Mechanical Design Engineer

Job Description:

As a Mechanical Design Engineer, you will be responsible for designing and developing mechanical systems and components. You will work closely with cross-functional teams to ensure that designs meet performance, reliability, and manufacturability standards.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Develop and create detailed mechanical designs using CAD software.
  • Conduct design reviews and collaborate with engineering teams to refine designs.
  • Perform simulations and analyses to validate design performance.
  • Create and maintain documentation for design specifications and changes.
  • Support prototype development and testing.

CAE Analyst

Number of Openings: 2

Designation: CAE Analyst

Job Description:

The CAE Analyst will focus on performing advanced simulations and analyses to support product development. You will utilize various CAE tools to assess structural integrity, thermal performance, and other critical parameters.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Conduct linear and non-linear finite element analysis (FEA) for various components.
  • Perform durability and fatigue analysis to ensure product longevity.
  • Collaborate with design engineers to optimize designs based on analysis results.
  • Prepare detailed reports and presentations of analysis findings.
  • Stay updated with the latest CAE tools and methodologies.

Electric Vehicle Engineer

Number of Openings: 2

Designation: Electric Vehicle Engineer

Job Description:

As an Electric Vehicle Engineer, you will be involved in the design and development of electric vehicle systems, including powertrains, battery management systems, and thermal management solutions.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Design and analyze electric vehicle components and systems.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to integrate electrical and mechanical systems.
  • Conduct performance testing and validation of electric vehicle prototypes.
  • Stay informed about industry trends and advancements in electric vehicle technology.
  • Support the development of technical documentation and specifications.

Tool Design Specialist

Number of Openings: 1

Designation: Tool Design Specialist

Job Description:

The Tool Design Specialist will be responsible for designing and developing tools and fixtures used in manufacturing processes. You will ensure that tools are efficient, reliable, and meet production requirements.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Design and develop tooling solutions for various manufacturing processes.
  • Collaborate with production teams to understand tooling requirements.
  • Conduct feasibility studies and cost analysis for tool designs.
  • Create detailed drawings and specifications for tool fabrication.
  • Support the implementation and testing of new tooling solutions.

Project Manager

Number of Openings: 1

Designation: Project Manager

Job Description:

The Project Manager will oversee engineering projects from inception to completion, ensuring that they are delivered on time, within scope, and within budget. You will coordinate with various teams and stakeholders to achieve project goals.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Develop project plans, timelines, and budgets.
  • Coordinate resources and manage project teams to ensure successful execution.
  • Monitor project progress and make adjustments as necessary.
  • Communicate project status to stakeholders and address any issues that arise.
  • Conduct post-project evaluations to identify areas for improvement.
