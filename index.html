Our Services

Design Services

  • Product Design: Transform your concepts into market-ready products with our innovative design solutions that prioritize functionality and aesthetics.
  • Structure Design: We specialize in creating robust structural designs that ensure safety and durability, tailored to meet industry standards and client specifications.
  • Chassis Design: Our team engineers lightweight yet strong chassis designs that enhance vehicle performance and safety, perfect for automotive and industrial applications.
  • Plastic Design: We excel in designing plastic components that are not only functional but also cost-effective, utilizing advanced materials and manufacturing techniques.
  • 3D Solid and Surface Modeling: Bring your ideas to life with our state-of-the-art 3D modeling services, allowing for precise visualization and modification before production.
  • Detailed Design: Our meticulous approach to detailed design ensures that every aspect of your project is accounted for, minimizing errors and maximizing efficiency.
  • CAD Customization: Tailor your CAD systems to fit your unique workflow with our customization services, enhancing productivity and design accuracy.
  • Parametric Part Modeling: We create flexible parametric models that adapt to design changes seamlessly, saving time and resources during the development process.
  • Reverse Engineering: Unlock the potential of existing products with our reverse engineering services, enabling you to analyze and improve upon current designs.

CAE Services

  • Linear and Non-linear Analysis: Our advanced analysis techniques help predict how products will perform under various conditions, ensuring reliability and safety.
  • Structural Analysis: We assess the integrity of structures through rigorous analysis, identifying potential weaknesses and optimizing designs for performance.
  • Structures and Systems Simulation: Simulate real-world conditions to evaluate the behavior of complex systems, allowing for informed decision-making and design improvements.
  • Plastics & Structures: Specialized analysis for plastic components and structures, ensuring they meet performance and durability standards.
  • Durability/Fatigue Analysis: We evaluate the lifespan of components under cyclic loading conditions, helping you design for longevity and reliability.
  • Forming Simulation: Optimize manufacturing processes with our forming simulation services, reducing waste and improving efficiency.
  • Modal Analysis: Understand the dynamic behavior of structures with our modal analysis, ensuring they perform as intended under various conditions.

Electric Vehicles

  • Staffing Solutions: We provide expert staffing solutions tailored to the electric vehicle sector, connecting you with top talent to drive your projects forward.
  • Embedded Engineering: Our embedded engineering services focus on developing sophisticated control systems for electric vehicles, enhancing performance and functionality.

Tool Engineering

  • Plate Type Design: We design efficient plate-type tools that streamline manufacturing processes, ensuring precision and reliability.
  • Progressive Tool Design: Our progressive tool designs facilitate high-volume production with minimal downtime, maximizing efficiency.
  • Forming Tool Design: We create innovative forming tools that enhance production capabilities while maintaining quality standards.
  • Try Out Simulation: Validate your tooling designs with our try-out simulation services, ensuring they perform as expected before full-scale production.
  • Die Simulation and Mold Flow Analysis: Optimize your die and mold designs with our simulation services, reducing defects and improving product quality.
  • Jigs & Fixture Design: We design custom jigs and fixtures that enhance manufacturing accuracy and efficiency, tailored to your specific needs.
  • SPM Design: Our Special Purpose Machine (SPM) design services focus on creating automated solutions that improve production efficiency and reduce labor costs.
